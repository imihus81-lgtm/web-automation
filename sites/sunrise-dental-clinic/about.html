About Sunrise Dental Clinic

Since our establishment in Tokyo, Sunrise Dental Clinic has been dedicated to bringing French dental excellence to Japan. Our team of skilled dental professionals, many trained in France, offers a comprehensive range of dental services with a strong focus on patient comfort and advanced treatments.

We pride ourselves on being a multicultural clinic with bilingual staff, allowing us to serve Tokyo’s diverse community while upholding the highest standards of care straight from France.

Our commitment is to create a relaxing, welcoming environment while ensuring the best possible results for every patient, whether you’re seeking preventive care, cosmetic improvements, or advanced restorative treatments.

Our Mission & Values

  • Delivering gentle, effective dental care in a caring environment.
  • Introducing the latest technologies and French techniques to Tokyo.
  • Building trusting relationships with our patients.
  • Continuously improving through education and innovation.
